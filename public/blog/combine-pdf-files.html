How to Combine PDF Files on Any Device

Published: May 27, 2025 | 7 min read | By Easy-PDF-Tools Team

Universal Method: Works on Every Device

The best way to combine PDF files on ANY device is using our web-based PDF merger tool. Since it runs in your browser, it works identically on Windows, Mac, Linux, Chromebook, iPhone, iPad, and Android devices.

Universal Access: No software installation needed. Works on any device with a modern web browser.

Step-by-Step (All Devices)

  1. Open your browser and go to our PDF merger tool
  2. Tap/click "Browse Files" or drag and drop your PDFs
  3. Select the files in the order you want them merged
  4. Click "Convert Now"
  5. Download your combined PDF

Device-Specific Tips

Windows / Mac / Linux

Drag and drop files directly from File Explorer/Finder into the drop zone. You can select multiple files at once using Ctrl+Click (Windows) or Cmd+Click (Mac).

iPhone / iPad (iOS)

Tap the "Browse Files" button, then select files from your Files app, Photos, or iCloud Drive. You can also select files from other apps like Google Drive or Dropbox.

Android

Tap "Browse Files" and select PDFs from your Downloads, Google Drive, or other storage locations. The interface adapts perfectly to your screen size.

Chromebook

Our tool works great on ChromeOS. Use the file picker to select PDFs from your Downloads folder or Google Drive.
