How to Reduce PDF File Size for Email

Published: May 27, 2025 | 7 min read | By Easy-Pdf-Tools Team

How to Compress Your PDF

  1. Visit our free PDF compressor
  2. Upload your PDF file
  3. Select your compression level (Low/Medium/High)
  4. Click "Convert Now"
  5. Download your compressed PDF and attach it to your email

Pro Tip: For email attachments, medium compression is usually enough to get under 25MB while maintaining good quality. Only use high compression if necessary.

Expected Compression Results

  • Scanned documents: 50-70% reduction (10MB → 3-5MB)
  • Image-heavy PDFs: 30-50% reduction (25MB → 12-17MB)
  • Text-only PDFs: 10-20% reduction (5MB → 4-4.5MB)

Alternative Methods

Before Creating the PDF

If you're creating the PDF from images, compress the images first using an image optimizer, then convert to PDF. This often yields better results than compressing the final PDF.

Split and Send

If compression isn't enough, use our split tool to divide the PDF into smaller parts and send multiple emails.

Use Cloud Storage

For files that remain too large even after compression, upload to Google Drive or Dropbox and share a link instead of attaching directly.
