About EasyPDF Tools

Established 2025

Our Mission

EasyPDF Tools was created with a simple mission: to provide free, secure, and easy-to-use PDF tools for everyone, everywhere. We believe that document conversion and editing should be accessible to all, without hidden costs, sign-ups, or complicated software.

100% Free Forever - We are committed to keeping all our PDF tools completely free with no premium tiers or hidden charges.

What We Offer

  • Merge PDF - Combine multiple PDFs into one document
  • Image to PDF - Convert JPG, PNG to PDF format
  • Compress PDF - Reduce PDF file size while maintaining quality
  • Add Watermark - Protect your documents with custom watermarks
  • Split PDF - Extract specific pages from PDF files
  • Word to PDF - Convert DOCX files to PDF format
  • PDF to Word - Extract text from PDF to DOCX

Why Choose EasyPDF Tools

  • Completely Free - No credit card, no trial, no limits
  • No Sign-up Required - Use instantly, no account needed
  • Privacy First - Files auto-delete after 60 minutes
  • SSL Encrypted - 256-bit security for all transfers
  • Works Everywhere - All devices and browsers supported
  • No Software Installation - Pure web-based solution

Our Story

EasyPDF Tools was born out of frustration with existing PDF tools that either charged high fees, required sign-ups, or had complicated interfaces. We set out to build a simple, easy-to-use solution that anyone could use for free.

Today, thousands of users trust EasyPDF Tools for their document needs. We're proud to offer a service that puts users first - no ads, no tracking, no BS. Just easy tools that work.
