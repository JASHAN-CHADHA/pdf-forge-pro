GDPR Compliance

Last updated: January 2025

EasyPDF Tools is fully compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) (EU) 2016/679. This page explains how we protect your data and your rights under GDPR.

1. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a user in the European Union, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access - Request what personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification - Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten) - Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Restrict Processing - Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability - Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object - Object to data processing for direct marketing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent - Withdraw consent at any time

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data to provide our service:

Data TypePurposeLegal Basis
Email AddressNewsletter (optional)Consent
CookiesAnalytics & AdvertisingLegitimate Interest
Usage DataService ImprovementLegitimate Interest

We DO NOT collect: Names, physical addresses, phone numbers, payment information, or file contents.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on:

  • Your consent - For newsletter subscriptions
  • Legitimate interests - For website analytics, security, and service improvement
  • Contractual necessity - To provide our PDF conversion service

4. Data Retention Period

  • Uploaded Files: Deleted within 60 minutes
  • Email Data: Retained until you unsubscribe
  • Cookies: 30 days maximum
  • Usage Logs: 30 days (anonymized)

5. Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA). We ensure adequate safeguards are in place, including standard contractual clauses approved by the European Commission.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics for anonymous usage statistics
  • Advertising Cookies: Google AdSense for personalized advertisements

You can manage cookie preferences through our cookie banner or your browser settings.

7. Data Controller Information

EasyPDF Tools is the data controller for personal data collected through our service.

Contact our Data Protection Officer (DPO): dpo@easypdftools.com

8. How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of your GDPR rights:

  1. Email us at: privacy@easypdftools.com
  2. Subject line: "GDPR Request"
  3. Include your email address and specific request
  4. We will respond within 30 days

9. Right to Lodge a Complaint

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority if you believe our processing of your personal data violates GDPR.

10. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal data, we will notify you within 72 hours as required by GDPR.
