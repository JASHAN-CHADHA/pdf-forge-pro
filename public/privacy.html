Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

PDFForge Pro does not collect or store any personal information unless voluntarily provided. We may collect:

  • Email address - Only when subscribing to our newsletter (optional)
  • Anonymous usage data - Through cookies for analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our PDF conversion services
  • To improve user experience and website performance
  • To send occasional updates (only with your consent)

3. File Privacy

All files uploaded to PDFForge Pro are automatically deleted from our servers within 60 minutes of conversion. We do not store, share, or access your files. Your privacy is our priority.

Your files are safe: 256-bit SSL encryption protects all file transfers. Files are processed in memory and never written to permanent storage.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to personalize ads and analyze site traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Google AdSense for advertising. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to our site and other sites on the internet.

6. Data Security

All file transfers use 256-bit SSL encryption. Your files are processed in memory and never stored permanently. We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service does not address anyone under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@pdfforge.com
